Step by Step Guide - Vacant Room

This guide outlines the tasks to be completed when preparing a vacant room for the next guest:

  1. Room Preparation

    Begin by preparing the room for cleaning and ensuring it is safe to enter:

    • Check Room Status: Ensure the room status indicates vacant and not under DND (Do Not Disturb).

    • Ring Bell and Announce: Ring the bell or knock on the door. Announce "Housekeeping" before entering.

    • Wait and Repeat Announcement: Wait for a response. If there is no response, repeat the announcement twice with a 10-second interval.

    • Unlock and Enter: Once acknowledged, unlock the door and open it slightly (about 30 degrees).

  2. Cleaning and Preparation Tasks

    Proceed with cleaning and preparing the room for the next guest's arrival:

    1. Bed Area

      • Strip Bed: Remove used linens and prepare to replace with fresh ones.

      • Make Bed: Make the bed with fresh linens and arrange pillows neatly.

    2. Furniture and Surfaces

      • Dust and Wipe Surfaces: Dust and wipe down all furniture surfaces, including desks, chairs, and bedside tables.

      • Arrange Items: Straighten up and arrange guest materials, magazines, and books.

    3. Bathroom Area

      • Clean and Disinfect: Clean and disinfect the sink, toilet, bathtub/shower, and countertops.

      • Restock Amenities: Ensure towels, toiletries, and amenities are fully stocked and replenished.

    4. Floor Area

      • Vacuum or Sweep: Vacuum carpets or sweep hard floors thoroughly.

      • Mop if Needed: Mop floors if necessary, ensuring they are clean and dry.

  3. Final Inspection and Preparation

    Perform a final inspection to ensure cleanliness and readiness for the next guest:

    • Check Windows and Curtains: Ensure windows are clean and curtains are straightened.

    • Check Lighting and Temperature: Ensure lighting is functional and adjust room temperature as needed.

    • Leave Welcome Note: Leave a note welcoming the next guest and indicating the room has been cleaned and prepared.

Following these steps ensures that each vacant room is thoroughly cleaned, arranged, and prepared to welcome the next guest.
