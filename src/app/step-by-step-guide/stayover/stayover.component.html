Step by Step Guide - Stayover

This guide provides a detailed approach for handling a stayover room, ensuring cleanliness and comfort for the guest:

  1. Knock and Announce

    Knock on the door and announce "Housekeeping". Wait for a response before entering.

  2. Greet Guest and Confirm Services

    Greet the guest warmly. Confirm which services are needed (e.g., towels changed, amenities restocked).

  3. Cleaning and Arranging Tasks

    1. Bed Area

      • Make the Bed: Strip the bed if necessary and replace with fresh linens.

      • Arrange Pillows: Fluff pillows and arrange neatly.

      • Check Headboard: Ensure headboard is clean and dust-free.

    2. Furniture and Surfaces

      • Dust Furniture: Dust all furniture surfaces, including desks, chairs, and bedside tables.

      • Wipe Surfaces: Clean and sanitize surfaces such as desks and bedside tables.

      • Arrange Items: Straighten up magazines, books, and guest materials.

    3. Bathroom Area

      • Clean and Disinfect: Clean and disinfect the sink, toilet, bathtub/shower, and countertops.

      • Restock Amenities: Ensure towels, toiletries, and amenities are replenished.

      • Arrange Towels: Fold and arrange fresh towels neatly.

    4. Floor Area

      • Vacuum or Sweep: Vacuum carpets or sweep hard floors thoroughly.

      • Mop if Needed: Mop floors if necessary, ensuring they are clean and dry.

      • Check for Debris: Ensure there are no debris or items left on the floor.

  4. Final Inspection and Guest Interaction

    Perform a final inspection to ensure everything is clean and arranged properly. Leave a note thanking the guest for their stay and indicating the room has been serviced.

Following these detailed steps ensures that each stayover room is meticulously cleaned, arranged, and prepared for the guest's comfort.
