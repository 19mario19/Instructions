Behaviour Guidelines

As a room attendant at COMO Metropolitan London, maintaining professional behavior at all times is crucial. Please adhere to the following behaviour guidelines:

  • Always greet guests with a warm and friendly smile.

  • Maintain a respectful distance and allow guests to pass in corridors and other public areas.

  • Be attentive and responsive to guests' needs and requests.

  • Refrain from engaging in lengthy conversations with guests unless necessary.

  • Avoid using mobile phones or other electronic devices while on duty, except for work-related purposes.

  • Respect guests' privacy and avoid entering rooms without permission.

  • Report any suspicious activity or incidents to the appropriate supervisor immediately.

  • Ensure all interactions with guests are courteous and professional.

Following these behaviour guidelines helps create a positive and welcoming environment for all guests at COMO Metropolitan London.
