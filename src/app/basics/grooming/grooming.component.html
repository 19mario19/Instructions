Grooming Standards

Ensuring a professional and clean appearance is essential for all room attendants at COMO Metropolitan London. Please adhere to the following grooming standards:

  • Uniforms should be clean and well-pressed at all times.

  • Hair should be neatly styled and kept away from the face. Long hair must be tied back.

  • Minimal jewelry is allowed; avoid wearing excessive or flashy accessories.

  • Fingernails should be kept clean and trimmed. Avoid brightly colored nail polish.

  • Personal hygiene must be maintained at all times, including regular hand washing and use of deodorant.

  • Footwear should be clean, comfortable, and appropriate for the work environment.

  • Makeup, if worn, should be subtle and natural-looking.

Adhering to these grooming standards helps maintain the high level of professionalism and cleanliness that guests expect at COMO Metropolitan London.
