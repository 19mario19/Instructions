Procedure Entering the Room

Follow this procedure when entering a guest room:

  1. Check DND (Do Not Disturb) Light: Ensure the DND light is not illuminated (not red).

  2. Ring Bell: Ring the bell at the door.

  3. Announce: Say "Housekeeping" loudly after ringing the bell.

  4. Wait: Wait for 10 seconds.

  5. Repeat: Repeat steps 2 and 3 two more times.

  6. Unlock and Open Door: Unlock the door and open it slightly to about 30 degrees.

  7. Announce Again: Say "Housekeeping" again after opening the door.

Following this procedure ensures proper communication with guests and respects their privacy.
