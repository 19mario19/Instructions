Cleaning Status - Linen Change

When a room has a linen change cleaning status, follow these guidelines to ensure it is thoroughly refreshed:

  • Enter the room and strip the bed, removing all used linens and pillowcases.

  • Replace with fresh linens and pillowcases, ensuring the bed is made neatly.

  • Replace used towels and toiletries.

  • Empty all trash bins and replace with clean liners.

  • Wipe down surfaces, such as the desk, nightstands, and countertops.

  • Ensure the bathroom is clean and stocked with necessary supplies.

  • Perform a thorough vacuum or sweep of the floors.

  • Check for any maintenance issues and report them promptly.

Following these guidelines ensures the room is thoroughly cleaned and refreshed for the guest.
