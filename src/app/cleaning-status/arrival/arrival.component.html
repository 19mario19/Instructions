Cleaning Status - Arrival

When a room is marked as arrival cleaning status, it is given priority and must be thoroughly cleaned and prepared for the new guest:

  • Enter the room and perform a thorough inspection to ensure no guest belongings are left behind.

  • Strip the bed and replace with fresh linens and pillowcases.

  • Replace used towels and toiletries.

  • Clean all surfaces, including furniture, mirrors, and windows.

  • Vacuum or mop the floors, ensuring no dirt or debris is left behind.

  • Restock all amenities, including toiletries, towels, and minibar items.

  • Empty all trash bins and replace with clean liners.

  • Check for any maintenance issues and report them promptly.

  • Ensure the room is set up according to the hotel's standards for new arrivals.

Following these guidelines ensures the room is thoroughly cleaned and ready for the incoming guest.
