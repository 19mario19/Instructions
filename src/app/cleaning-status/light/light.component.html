Cleaning Status - Light

When a room has a light cleaning status, follow these guidelines to ensure it is refreshed and presentable:

  • Enter the room and perform a general tidy-up, ensuring everything is in its place.

  • Replace used towels and toiletries.

  • Empty all trash bins and replace with clean liners.

  • Make the bed, ensuring it looks neat and inviting.

  • Wipe down surfaces, such as the desk, nightstands, and countertops.

  • Ensure the bathroom is clean and stocked with necessary supplies.

  • Perform a quick vacuum or sweep of the floors, if needed.

Following these guidelines ensures the room remains clean and comfortable for the guest.
