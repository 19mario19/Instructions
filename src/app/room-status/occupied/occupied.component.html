Room Status - Occupied

When a room is occupied, follow these guidelines to ensure the comfort and privacy of our guests:

  • Knock and announce yourself before entering the room.

  • Wait for a response before entering. If there is no response, knock again and announce yourself once more.

  • If the guest is present, ask if it is a convenient time to clean the room or if they prefer you to return later.

  • Perform the cleaning tasks efficiently and quietly to minimize disruption.

  • Ensure guest belongings are not disturbed and maintain their privacy at all times.

  • Replace used towels and toiletries, make the bed, and empty trash bins.

  • Check for any maintenance issues and report them promptly.

  • Leave a note informing the guest that the room has been serviced.

Following these guidelines helps ensure a pleasant and respectful experience for our guests.
