Products and Tools - List of Products

Below is a comprehensive list of cleaning products used by room attendants. Ensure you are familiar with each product and its proper use:

  • All-Purpose Cleaner: For cleaning various surfaces such as countertops, desks, and tables.

  • Glass Cleaner: For cleaning mirrors and windows to ensure they are streak-free.

  • Bathroom Cleaner: For cleaning sinks, toilets, and tubs, ensuring all fixtures are sanitized.

  • Disinfectant Spray: For sanitizing high-touch areas such as door handles, light switches, and remote controls.

  • Furniture Polish: For polishing wooden furniture and surfaces, providing a clean and polished finish.

  • Floor Cleaner: For mopping floors, ensuring they are clean and free from dirt and stains.

  • Air Freshener: For refreshing the room and eliminating any unpleasant odors.

  • Toilet Bowl Cleaner: For ensuring the toilet bowl is clean and sanitized.

  • Stain Remover: For treating any stains on carpets, upholstery, or linens.

Ensure you follow the usage instructions for each product and wear appropriate protective gear when necessary.
