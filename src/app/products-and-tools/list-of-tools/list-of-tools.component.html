Products and Tools - List of Tools

Below is a list of essential tools used by room attendants to ensure effective cleaning and maintenance of guest rooms:

  • Vacuum Cleaner: Used for cleaning floors, carpets, and upholstery.

  • Mop and Bucket: Used for mopping floors and ensuring they are clean and sanitized.

  • Broom and Dustpan: Used for sweeping and collecting debris from floors.

  • Dust Cloth and Dusters: Used for dusting surfaces and furniture.

  • Scrubbing Brushes and Sponges: Used for scrubbing and cleaning surfaces, including sinks and tubs.

  • Gloves and Protective Gear: Used for protection when handling cleaning chemicals and potentially hazardous materials.

  • Trash Bags and Bins: Used for collecting and disposing of trash from guest rooms.

  • Cleaning Caddy or Cart: Used for organizing and carrying cleaning supplies from room to room.

  • Toolbox with Basic Maintenance Tools: Used for addressing minor maintenance issues, such as replacing light bulbs or tightening loose fixtures.

Ensure all tools are properly maintained and stored after each use to ensure longevity and effectiveness.
